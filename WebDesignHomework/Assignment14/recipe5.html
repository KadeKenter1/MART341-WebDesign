Vegetarian Pesto Pasta

A quick, simple, and delicious vegetarian meal with a flavorful pesto sauce.

Ingredients Needed

  • 12 ounces of spaghetti or linguine
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts or walnuts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: cherry tomatoes for garnish

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the Pasta

Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti or linguine until al dente, then drain.

Step 2

Prepare the Pesto

In a blender or food processor, combine basil, Parmesan, olive oil, garlic, pine nuts, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.

Step 3

Combine Pasta and Pesto

In a large bowl, mix the pasta with the pesto sauce until evenly coated.

Step 4

Serve and Garnish

Serve in bowls, optionally garnishing with cherry tomatoes and extra Parmesan cheese.
