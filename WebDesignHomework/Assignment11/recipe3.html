Simple Vegetarian Pasta

A quick and easy vegetarian recipe perfect for any weeknight dinner.

Ingredients Needed

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the Spaghetti

Boil the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2

Sauté the Garlic

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

Step 3

Add the Tomatoes

Add cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes.

Step 4

Combine Spaghetti

Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat evenly. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 5

Serve

Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve hot!
