Vegan Lentil Soup

A hearty and nourishing vegan soup for any season!

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 cup dried lentils (green or brown)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups spinach (optional)

Instructions

Step 1

Prepare Your Ingredients

Rinse the lentils and chop all the vegetables.

Step 2

Sauté the Vegetables

In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery, and sauté for about 5 minutes, until softened.

Step 3

Add the Broth and Spices

Add the vegetable broth, tomatoes, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir well.

Step 4

Simmer the Soup

Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes until the lentils are tender.

Step 5

Add the Spinach

Stir in the spinach and let it wilt in the soup for another 5 minutes.

Step 6

Serve and Enjoy!

Serve hot and enjoy your warm, hearty vegan soup!
